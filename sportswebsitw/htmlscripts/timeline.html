Usain Bolts Timeline

  • Aug 21, 1986
    Born in Sherwood Content, Trelawny, Jamaica. 21 Aug 1986. Usain was born to parents Wellesley and Jennifer Bolt
  • 7 May 1999
    Trelawny Parish Run Usain Run Usain competed for his church in a local track meet. This is where people first realize that he was a good sprinter.
  • 15 Jun 2002
    Kingston Young Star He got his first national attention at the Junior World Championships. He got a gold in the 200 meter dash and two silvers in the 4x100 and 4x400.
  • 20 Aug 2003
    Italy Just Another Gold Usain won another gold medal in the 200 meter dash at the World Youth Championships.
  • 9 Aug 2004 Athens Injury:( Usain got put on the Jamaican Olympic Team but due to injury he was knocked out of the 200 meter preliminarys with a time of 21.05 seconds.
  • 31 May 2008 New York, NY Record Breaking Performance Bolt broke the world record time of 9.74 in the 100 meter dash with a time of 9.72. He did this at the Reebok Grand Prix held in New York City.
  • 18 Aug 2009 Beijing THE FASTEST MAN IN THE WORLD In the finals of the 100 meter Usain broke his own world record with a time of 9.58 secounds.
  • 11 Sep 2010 United States Gatorade Usain Bolt releases his new flavor of Gatorade called Bolt Lemon Ice.
